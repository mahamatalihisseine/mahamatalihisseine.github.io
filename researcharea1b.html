Research Area: Blockchain

Overview

My research in Blockchain explores its role as a foundation for trustworthy, decentralized digital ecosystems. From digital identities and credential verification to supply chain traceability, I focus on harnessing Blockchain technologies for integrity, transparency, and resilience across industries and borders.

Research Focus Areas

  • Decentralized Identity & Persistent Identifiers (DIDs)
  • Smart Contract Development & Governance Frameworks (e.g., DAO)
  • Distributed Systems & IPFS-Based Storage Solutions
  • Trust Frameworks for Supply Chain and Industrial IoT

Technologies & Frameworks

My work involves leveraging technologies such as Hyperledger Fabric, Solidity, Handle System, and IPFS to design, implement, and evaluate secure and resilient digital platforms. These platforms enable trust and accountability in multi-stakeholder environments, ensuring long-term reliability and accessibility of digital resources.
