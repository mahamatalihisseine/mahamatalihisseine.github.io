Agriculture and Food Traceability based Blockchain- Handle System

Overview

Blockchain and the Handle System enable end‑to‑end traceability across the agriculture and food supply chain. By linking customer data with blockchain‑based records — including farm‑to‑fork information, certifications, and quality data — stakeholders can verify the origin, production methods, and safety of food products. This allows customers to access trustworthy information, ensuring transparency and integrity across the supply chain from farm, processor, and retailer to consumer.

Key Benefits

Farm-to-Fork Traceability

Verify the source and quality of agriculture products, ensuring accountability from planting to delivery.

Certification Verification

Check certifications (organic, fair trade, etc.) linked securely via Handle identifiers and stored on the blockchain.

Data Transparency

All data about planting, harvesting, processing, and transport is securely stored and easily retrievable for auditing and quality control.

Consumer Trust

Customers can confidently make purchases based on trustworthy, verifiable information about food origins and quality.

Technologies Used

  • Blockchain for immutability and data verification
  • Handle System for persistent identifier assignment and access
  • Decentralized Identity (DIDs) for trusted access and data privacy
  • Smart Contracts for automating certifications and quality verification

Summary

By utilizing Blockchain and the Handle System, this initiative provides a trustworthy infrastructure for agriculture and food traceability. From farms to end‑consumers, every piece of information — including certifications, quality data, and transaction history — is stored and linked reliably. This empowers farmers, suppliers, retailers, and customers with a seamless, secure, and transparent ecosystem for sustainable agriculture and trustworthy food supply chains.
