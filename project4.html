E-Government Platform Using Blockchain and Handle System

Overview

This project introduces a next-generation e-government model using Blockchain and the Handle System. It facilitates secure, cross-departmental data sharing while preserving citizen privacy through decentralized identifiers and Digital Object Architecture (DOA). The platform supports coordinated access to healthcare,social services, emergency assistance, and tax verification by providing tamper-proof, persistent identifiers to citizen records.

Departmental Collaboration

Health Department

Manages digital health records using DOA and assigns Handle IDs to each citizen for secure record access and storage.

Social Services

Works with the Health Department to identify vulnerable populations and provide coordinated support and eligibility assessments.

Public Health Agency

Analyzes anonymized health data for outbreak detection and initiates interventions in collaboration with health officials.

Emergency Services

Accesses up-to-date health records using Handle identifiers to respond swiftly to medical emergencies and disasters.

Finance & Taxation

Verifies healthcare expenses using DOA to ensure proper tax deductions and healthcare-related financial support.

Core Technologies

  • Blockchain: Tamper-proof transaction history with document hashing and timestamping.
  • Handle System: Persistent digital identifiers for linking and accessing records securely.
  • Digital Object Architecture (DOA): Enables modular, distributed record access and privacy control.
  • Smart Contracts: Rules-based access control between departments.

Summary

This blockchain-based e-government platform transforms how departments collaborate around citizen data. It ensures citizens receive comprehensive services while maintaining privacy and compliance. With persistent identifiers, decentralized access control, and smart contracts, governments can respond faster, reduce duplication, and build citizen trust.
