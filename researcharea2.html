Research Area: Handle System

Overview

The Handle System is a robust framework for creating, managing, and resolving unique digital identifiers. This research explores its application for persistent identifier services, enabling trust, transparency, and long‑term accessibility across digital environments such as academic diplomas, digital archives, and supply chains.

Research Focus Areas

  • Persistent Identifiers (PIDs) for Digital Objects and Credentials
  • Decentralized Resolution Architecture for Trust Frameworks
  • Enhancing Trust and Verifiability for Diplomas, Certificates, and IP Assets

Technologies & Frameworks

Leveraging the Handle System for unique digital identifiers, combined with technologies like Blockchain, IPFS, and DIDs to create resilient, trustworthy digital platforms for long‑term accessibility and verification across industries and disciplines.

Selected Projects & Publications

  • Blockchain based handle system to secure Predictive maintenance analysis system in Industrial IoT using L2S–GRU (2025)
  • Enhancing Handle System for Digital Credentials Verification by Using Blockchain (2025)
